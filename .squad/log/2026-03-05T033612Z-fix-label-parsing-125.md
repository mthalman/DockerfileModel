**Solution:** Added context-aware `ExceptQuote` method and updated `WrappedInQuotesLiteralString` and `WrappedInQuotesIdentifier` to exclude only the wrapping quote character. Fix is minimal, backward compatible, and affects all instructions with quoted values.
